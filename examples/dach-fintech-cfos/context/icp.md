## Product / value proposition
We sell **RegLedger**, a compliance-automation platform that turns scattered financial
controls into one audited, regulator-ready system. It cuts month-end close risk and makes
SOC 2 / DORA / MiCA evidence collection continuous instead of a fire drill. The wedge:
finance leaders at fast-scaling fintechs who just hit the headcount/funding stage where
manual controls break.

## Target market segments (priority order)
1. **Mid-market fintechs, 200–2,000 employees, in DACH** with a dedicated finance/RevOps
   function and recent funding (Series B+). Primary.
2. Adjacent: regulated payments / lending / treasury startups in DACH, 50–200 employees.
3. Long-shot: non-DACH EU fintechs with a DACH entity.

## Default geography
DACH (Germany, Austria, Switzerland).

## Default seniorities
CxO, VP, Director.

## Seed / exemplar companies (for look-alike discovery)
Nimbus Pay, Helvetia Ledger, Tirol Treasury, Rheinmetrik Finance.

## Anti-fit signals
Too small (<50 employees), no finance function, pure crypto-native with no fiat controls,
already standardized on a Big-4-built internal controls stack we can't displace.
